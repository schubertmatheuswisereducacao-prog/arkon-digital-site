FAQ

Common Questions.

How long does it take to get my website live?

Once you fill out our onboarding form, we typically have a first draft ready for your review within 3-5 business days. After your approval, we go live immediately.

Do I need to prepare anything?

Just basic info about your business (services, hours, contact info). If you have a logo and photos, great! If not, we can use high-quality stock images that fit your brand perfectly.

Can I make changes after the site is live?

Absolutely. Minor text and image updates are included in your $40/month fee. Just email or WhatsApp us, and we handle the updates so you don't have to learn any complex software.

When do I start paying the $40/month?

Your first monthly payment is charged 30 days after you sign up. The $300 setup fee is the only thing due today.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, there are no long-term contracts. You can cancel your monthly subscription at any time.
